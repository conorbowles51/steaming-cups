Our story

A little kiosk with big energy.

We set up Steaming Cups at the entrance to Drumcondra Station — a small spot with a big commitment to doing coffee properly. Every morning, commuters and students heading to DCU know exactly where to stop.

Our beans are locally roasted, ground to order, and pulled fresh for every cup. We use ethically sourced cacao, locally farmed whole milk, and steam everything to a silky, quiet spin — the kind that leaves a proper rosetta on a flat white and makes a cappuccino stand up on its own.

The pastry case fills up before dawn — everything organic, everything baked fresh. We keep things small on purpose — it's the only way a café stays a café.
